About Us

This is what we do

Welcome to Books On The Go. A quick and easy way to buy and sell your Baruch textbooks.

Based on the current trend, we have noticed that students are increasingly using e-textbooks, pdfs or buying textbooks online for their classes. It is easier for students to get their textbooks online versus physically going to a store to obtain the required textbooks.

Further, we have also noticed that Baruch students lack a platform where they can communicate with their fellow peers and have discussions in regards to all aspects of their educational experience at Baruch College.

Our website gives students access to textbooks as well a platform to connect with other Baruch students. Students will have the ability to buy textbooks and sell their old textbooks.

Our goal is to provide a specialized website that caters to the direct needs and wants of Baruch students, by providing them access to textbooks and platform to connect with fellow Baruch students.
